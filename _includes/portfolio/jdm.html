JustDelete.me started as a weekend project between Ed Poole and myself to create a directory of direct links to account deletion pages for web services. The site launched with 16 services. Within four days of launching the site, we'd been picked up by Wired and five days later we hit one million page views.

The site now lists over 350 services, a Chrome extension and has been partially translated into 18 languages. Here's some completely not-out-of-context-at-all quotes:
